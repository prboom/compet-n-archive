Your DOOM config (key bindings, controllers):
S = Run, A = SL, D = SR, < = Back (< on a Swedish keyboard is placed between left shift and Z), Space = Use, LMB = Fire/Punch, MMB = Strafeon and RMB = Forward
Size of DOOM directory on your HD:
Over 300 MB with all external wads :) (I will however delete Doom as soon as the latest project I am involved in is finished, that´s the only way for me to stop this crazy thing. :)
First contact to COMPET-N (when, how etc.):
Late 95 or early 96 when I got Internet, I didn´t know until then you could record demos and I downloaded some lmps by Andi Kren, DL, Winterfeldt, Dasa and Kai-Uwe and was stunned, I started playing like mad after this..
First COMPET-N demo:
F23-2248 in May 96 :)
Favourite COMPET-N demo(s) from yourself (the one(s) that you're most proud of):
Maybe Final Doom Evilution map14 on NM, some Max demos from Doom2 and My Tyson TGM which are not of course Compet-n.
Favourite COMPET-N demo(s) from others:
Demos by Istvan and Andi (their d2 nm runs). Speed: Demos by Adam, Esko (map24 and those in the connected speedrun), Kai and Yonatan. Max: Demos by DL (I haven´t checked the latest records in either category though)
Favourite DOOM or DOOM2 map:
Doom2 Map14
Favourite TC/megawad:
HR and MM

I still think it´s fun, but it has sort of died, don´t think I´ll ever record anything besides this last project I´m involved with (a new MM2 collection together with the Norwegians)..
Anything you want to add here:
I would say in general that Tyson is much underestimated, with -fast monsters it is probably the hardest thing to do, it should have been a part of Compet-n.. I would also like to say goodbye to all friends I have met through the years playing Doom..

Known as the third player in the Nightmare triumvirate besides Anthe and Istvan :) Peo is a huge all-around player, among the best in all DOOMing styles - Nightmare, Max, Tyson, Speed.
